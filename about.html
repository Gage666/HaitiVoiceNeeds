About HaitiVoiceNeeds (HVN) Project

This site was developed during the CrisisCampBoston event on January 23, 2010 to address the last mile bottleneck of capturing the needs of the Haitian people after the earthquake. In particular, it avoids the need for computerized data entry systems on the ground, and instead allows Haitians to state their needs using nothing more than a regular phone call and their voice. We take transcriptions of calls and pass them on to data aggregators who help co-ordinate the response on the ground in Haiti.

You can read more about the development of the project from the HaitiVoiceNeeds wiki page.
