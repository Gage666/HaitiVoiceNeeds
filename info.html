Haiti Voice Needs is a service that lets earthquake survivors in Haiti make requests and provide information just by making a phone call. We have a toll-free number, +1 (877) 286-0676 that Haitians can call and record updates. We then translate and transcribe the Haitian creole into english and pass the information on to data aggregators such as Ushahidi and Google Person Finder.

Our transcription is done by bilingual volunteers. We have an interface that gives a translator a Creole audio clip and a place to enter the transcription. We need Creole speakers to volunteer just a couple minutes of their time to transcribe one or two messages-the information they translate could save lives! You can help out right now by transcribing some of the messages at the bottom of this page

We've also created an API to open our service up to developers. We're set up to do crowdsourced transcription of Creole recordings. We have an simple, free way for you to get quality transcriptions of your recordings. Click here to see our API spec and examples of how to use it.
