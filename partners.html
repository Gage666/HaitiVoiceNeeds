• Ushahidia
    Ushahidi tracks events happening in Haiti in real-time. We pass our transcribed calls on to them, and they take the information, combine it with tweets and other info coming out of Haiti, and make it available in rel-time to groups on the ground in Haiti
  • Haiti Earthquake.tel
    Haiti Earthquake.tel is a centralized resource of information about what's going on in Haiti, phone numbers people can call to get or give information and links to other projects addressing what's going on right now in Haiti
